Steven A. Rodríguez is a social entreprenur and growth marketing strategist with a passion for leadership through personal and professional development.

A rising star awardee and graduate of CUNY The City University of New York, Steven has taken his pursuit of global impact into the world of tech and entrepreneurship.

Steven is an active partner in multiple companies, including an operations LatAm talent firm, a business growth consultancy cooperative, and a venture studio organization driving innovation for a better future.

As an OpenExO Sprint Coach, Steven's mission is to level the emerging playing tech field field for diverse small business owners, and regularly travels the globe as an entrepreneurial advocate.
